What is a “Main”?

Overwatch is a captivating first person shooter with 24 unique heroes to choose from. Each hero has their own strengths and weaknesses making every person favor a specific hero. This hero is known in the community as their "main" hero. It is possible to have a few main heros. There are 4 categories of characters available: Offense, Defense, Tank. and Healer. Offense is characters who get up close and personal and deal damage. Defense characters hang back and pick off enemies. Tanks have increased health and are meant to protect their team. And lastly healer have low damage output but can regenerate their teammate health. View this link to see the ablities for each character. Below is a picture of each hero.

What is Overwatch?

Overwatch is a fast paced first person shooter game available on PC and Console. The team is composed of 6 people versus another team of 6 and you must complete an objective based task quicker than your team. This consists of pushing a cart a certain distance, controlling a point, attacking a point while the other defend, or even a combination of these in hybrid game modes. Based on the game mode you will be placed on map with that game type and attempt to kill as many enemy team mates as possible while not dying and achieving your own objective.

What is Toxicity?

Toxicity is state of mind present in a lot of video games. When you are playing any video game that excites you, you should feel some anger when losing to make winning that much more exciting. You can hear teens use words like "salty" to express people who get angry after losing a video game, and this salt has to be present to actually enjoy winning. Toxicity is different than salt because being toxic is being so upset you negatively affect your teammates. Overwatch being a cooperative game, you must work together with your teammates to win and bad teammates= a loss. An example of a bad teammate is someone who yells at their team, gives bad advice, and doesn’t work well with others. The toxicity epidemic is the wave of toxic behavior that one transfers to another until everyone who plays this game will treat you poorly.
